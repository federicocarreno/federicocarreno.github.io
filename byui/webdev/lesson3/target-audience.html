Description of the target audience

The site will be aimed at users aged 16 to 45, mainly those who are looking for fun activities, both outdoors and in their own homes.

For example, if tomorrow the forecast indicates that there will be a great snowfall, it will suggest activities such as going skiing, giving you information about ski instructors, the best slopes, and much more!
