Site Name

Weather Partner Me

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to turn the state of the weather into your adventure companion. What does this mean? If you want to go out, or do something fun at home, on this site you will find ideas and suggestions for activities to do based on the weather of the day.

For example, if tomorrow the forecast indicates that there will be a great snowfall, it will suggest activities such as going skiing, giving you information about ski instructors, the best slopes, and much more!
