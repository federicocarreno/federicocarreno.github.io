Visual Hierarchy

SPARK Digital

sparkdigital.com

In this example, we can clearly see the hierarchical levels of information. Due to the size, weight of the typeface, and the blue background that frames everything, without a doubt that the main message captures the first attention. The other elements are quickly sorted by size and location, such as the logo at the top, the "Learn more" button at the bottom, and the menu at the top right.

White Space and Clean Design

IBM

ibm.com/design/teams

Here we can see an excellent example of pure and clean design. The simple and pure black line contrasts perfectly with the white background. The icons, with the same stroke of the text, also contain only the graphic information necessary to understand their meaning. Simplicity in its purest expression, creating a new beauty.

Repetition

Mercado Libre

mercadolibre.com.ar

Here, the repetition of elements, allows us to quickly sort and select the available categories. In turn, not only the frames are repeated, but also the stroke and color of the icons, typography, padding, etc.
